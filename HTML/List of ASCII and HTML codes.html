Codes in the decimal range 128 to 159 are coded as C1 control characters
For use with Flanagan's Library. Strings class
A version of this table, in which some of the codes in the decimal range 128 to 159 are used to code for printable characters following CP-1252 encoding, may be found on HTMLandASCIItableWin.html
A version of this table, in which the codes in the decimal range 128 to 159 are not used, may be found on HTMLandASCIItableIso.html
Control characters are indicated as, e.g. ^C or EscC, where the ^ or the Esc indicates that the key ^ or Esc must be depressed at the same time as the following character, e.g. C, key.
